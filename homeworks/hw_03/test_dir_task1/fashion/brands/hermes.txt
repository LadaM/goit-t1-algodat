Established in 1837, Hermès has a history of over 185 years.
In the ateliers of Paris, Hermès crafted a legacy of exquisite craftsmanship and luxury. 
The brand's commitment to quality leather goods, including the coveted Birkin and Kelly bags, has made it a symbol of refined elegance. 
Jean-Paul Gaultier, during his tenure from 2003 to 2010, added his unique flair to the brand's heritage.
