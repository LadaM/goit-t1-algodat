Founded in 1909 by Coco Chanel, the brand has a history of over 110 years.
Amidst the chic boulevards of Paris, Coco Chanel introduced a brand synonymous with timeless elegance. The founder's iconic tweed suits and little black dresses became fashion staples. 
In the 1980s, Karl Lagerfeld breathed new life into Chanel, maintaining its classic allure while infusing a modern aesthetic.
