Established in 1913 by Mario Prada, Prada has been around for over a century.
In Milan, the Prada legacy unfolded, characterized by avant-garde designs and a commitment to quality. Miuccia Prada, granddaughter of the founder, emerged as a design maven, blending innovation with minimalism. 
Her influence from the late 1970s onwards has made Prada a symbol of contemporary elegance.
