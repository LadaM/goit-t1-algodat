21st Century: Globalization and Diversity (2000s Onward):

Female Fashion:
The 21st century has seen a diverse range of styles, influenced by globalization and the rise of fast fashion. Skinny jeans, athleisure, and eclectic mixes of vintage and modern elements characterize women's fashion. Sustainable and ethical fashion has gained prominence.

Male Fashion:
Men's fashion in the 21st century is marked by a mix of traditional tailoring and casual wear. Streetwear, sneaker culture, and gender-fluid styles have become mainstream. Sustainability and inclusivity are important considerations.

Highlights:

Revolutionary Garment: The rise of sustainable and inclusive fashion movements has challenged traditional norms, emphasizing individuality and responsible consumption.
